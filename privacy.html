Smart Recorder ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Audio Recordings: Voice recordings you create using the app are stored locally on your device.
  • Transcriptions: Text transcriptions of your recordings are processed and stored locally.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality and crash reporting.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide voice recording and transcription services
  • Store and organize your recordings locally
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements (via Google AdMob)
  • Process in-app purchases

3. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage: All your recordings and transcriptions are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your audio files on our servers.

On-Device Processing: Transcription is performed entirely on your device using the Whisper AI model. No audio data is sent to external servers for processing.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers for personalized ads.
  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases securely.
  • Whisper AI (On-Device): For speech-to-text transcription, processed entirely on your device.

5. Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • Microphone: Required to record audio
  • Storage: Required to save and access recordings
  • Internet: Required for ads, model download, and updates
  • Notifications: For recording status and reminders

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your recordings and data at any time through the app
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising in your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
