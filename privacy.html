Information Collection and Use

The Application collects potential personally identifiable information and audio data solely for the purpose of providing its core functionality (Recording and Transcription).

1. Audio Recordings: The Application records audio via the microphone only when you explicitly start a recording. These audio files are stored locally on your device.

2. Transcription Data: The Application uses on-device AI technology (Whisper) to transcribe your audio. Your audio recordings are NOT uploaded to any server for transcription; all processing happens locally on your phone to ensure privacy.

3. Third Party Services: The Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you to support advertising and improvement of the app.
